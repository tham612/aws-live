About Us

For many organizations, having a strong communication strategy is one of the most important parts of making sure employees are on the same page. There are many solutions available from emails, phone calls, voice mails, group meetings, and mobile apps, but SMS is something many organizations don't consider. A mass employee SMS alert is an effective and reliable way to communicate information, and Textedly makes it easy to send and keep track of the texts.
